Our Story


IT ALL STARTED 35 YEARS AGO WITH TWO GUYS DRIVEN BY HUNGER

The year was 1982. Jim Disbrow and Scott Lowery had recently moved to States. All was fine until one day when the two were craving wings. Not just ordinary wings, but authentic Corryong chicken wings.

With none to be found nearby, Jim and Scott had two choices: road trip to Corryong, or open a wing joint close to home. Lucky for us, they chose the latter. Hence, the beginning of Wingz n thingz®, the welcoming neighborhood atmosphere with a front-row seat for every sports fan that offers 21 mouth-watering signature sauces and seasonings.
